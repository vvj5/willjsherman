MY STORY

Originally from Austin, Texas, I am a colourful guy with a wealth of worldly experience. My hard-earned combination of complimentary skillsets make me an unusually versatile and effective asset, and I bring a fresh perspective and creative spark to any organisation.

I started my professional career in Sydney as a graphic designer (first in print, then moving into web UI), adding UX design a few years later when I started my own creative services business.

While running my own business, I continued to hone all of my skillsets, and added Client-Facing Account Management, Agency-Side Projects and Project Management to my arsenal.

Later-on I took a role as Australian Marketing Manager for a Fortune 500 global industrial company, Genie Industries, and while doing so levelled-up my badge count with Corporate Events, Art Direction and Executive-Level Management.

In 2008 I returned to Austin, Texas and started my own consultancy, serving clients mainly with marketing strategy, various production projects and lots of events, including SXSW and a great deal to do with the locally-exploding craft brewery market.

With the birth of our first son in 2013, I took-on the hardest job of my life: Stay-At-Home-Daddy.

Three years later, I did a 12 week coding bootcamp, added web app development familiarity and a SCRUM PSM certification to my toolbox, and once more started my own consultancy.

The clients I worked with were very diverse, and my skills were constantly put to the test on very comprehensive, creative and technical projects.

For more about my projects and processes, please see the Case Studies section of my site.

My strongest performances come when I am working in a collaborative, vibrant environment, where ideas can be proposed and developed in an atmosphere of tolerance, respect and creativity.

Though I do take my work seriously, I don't take myself too seriously, and I truly love what I do.

Thanks for reading!

~Will
